Italian-sub

Here we have a classical, cheap and easy sub to make.

Make sure you get high quality deli meat, vegetables and bread, mm bread.

Ingredients

  • 2 (7- to 8-inch) hoagie rolls
  • 6 thin slices deli ham
  • 6 thin slices Genoa salami
  • 6 thin slices capicola ham
  • 2 slices provolone cheese, halved
  • 1/3 cup sliced banana peppers, or to taste
  • tomato slices, to taste
  • shredded lettuce
  • thinly sliced red onion
  • drizzle of olive oil
  • drizzle of red wine vinega
  • 1 pinch oregano
  • alt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
