Delicious Crockpot Meatballs

Meatball

Easy to make meatballs with no effort at all!

Ingrdients

  • Ground beef
  • Breading, seasoning
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Take your beef out and turn your oven on to 350
  2. Mix your beef, breading, seasoning and eggs together, shape into balls or cook as whole
  3. put on a tray and cook away!
