Crockpot Meatballs

Easy to make, delicious tasting with my own unique spin.

Skip the hassle of worrying about what to make and give it a shot!

Ingredients

  • 85/15 ground beef
  • my personal blend of spices
  • some grape jelly

Steps

  1. Mix all the spices together
  2. take our your ground beef and mix your spices together
  3. mix into ball shapes and put in your crockpot on high for 2 hours or low on 4
