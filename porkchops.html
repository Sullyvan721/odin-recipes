Porkchops

Easy cooked porkchops!

Oven baked porkchops quick and easy!

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless porkchops
  • Mayo, mustard or some kind of binder
  • Dan'O seasoning or seasoning of your choice

Directons

  1. preheat your oven to 350
  2. Bring your meat out and let it get to room temp
  3. In a bowl, combine all your spices with a binder of your choice, mix it with your meat and let that rest for a few hours
  4. Place on a tray and bake for 20-25 mintues
